Education about Web Technology

The purpose of this page is promote awareness of the information exchanged online. What is shown here is not acquired through any gimmickry or elaborate hacking. It relies on a regular function as a service (FaaS) hosted by a regular Cloud provider. What you see below is the information you, yourself, provide everytime you click on a link. In my oppinion, this should be discussed without alarm or libel, as education about the nature of the Web as a global village. That is, a place where we are not annonymous, which diminishes our privacy, but also creates entirely new opportunities for collective awareness.
